Class Practice Problem Guidelines

During the semester, I will distribute practice problems to reinforce your knowledge of the materials covered. You should attempt to answer all of the problems. Many of problems are best done using Excel, and I highly encourage you to improve your basic Excel skills, especially if you haven't worked with it much before. Note, your grade will not depend on your Excel skills, but it's a useful business skill to develop. I'm happy to distribute some suggestions of how I set up some of the problems in Excel.


Since we will not cover all of the problems, I will let you know beforehand which ones we will focus on in class. Some of the problems simply will ask for a formula, e.g., the present value of an annuity. We won't devote any class time to those. The more detailed ones will require you to apply concepts or formulas that we've covered to some real world facts.


I encourage you to experiment with LLMs, e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, in answering the problems. It is important to explore the benefits and limitations of LLMs. You'll see that many of the numerical answers given by LLMs to the problems are incorrect. C'est la vie.
